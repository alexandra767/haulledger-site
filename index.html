I.The thesis

Most tax apps don't speak truck.

Generic tax trackers ask you to pick a "vehicle" and slap on the IRS standard mileage rate. Class-8 trucks don't qualify — you have to use the actual expense method. HaulLedger is built around it from the first screen: every gallon, every tire, every quarter-IFTA mile, every overnight, all in one place.

  • ▸Built for the actual-expense method. No standard mileage trap.
  • ▸Designed for owner-operators, lease purchase, and 1099 company drivers.
  • ▸Settlement statement parser remembers the line items your broker or carrier always sends.
  • ▸Per-load profit, cost-per-mile, and net margin — not just gross revenue.

III.The biggest deduction

$69 a day. Every overnight away.

The IRS lets transportation workers under hours-of-service rules deduct $69 per day for meals and incidental expenses on overnights away from your tax home — at 80% deductibility. HaulLedger logs the overnight automatically and keeps the running total visible all year, not just at tax time.

  • ▸Overnight detection — full-day, partial-day (¾ rate), and home-day handled by the IRS rules.
  • ▸Running deduction counter, refreshed every settlement week.
  • ▸Editable: mark a stop as personal travel and we drop it from the count.
  • ▸One tap to export the year's overnight log as a CSV for your CPA.

Every receipt, filed by category.

  • ▸Receipt scanner — OCR on-device, no upload required.

V.Yours, only

Your books, not ours.

Every settlement, every receipt, every mile is stored on your iPhone with SwiftData. Optional iCloud sync uses your iCloud — Apple's encrypted infrastructure, not a HaulLedger server in the middle. Sign in with Apple is supported if you want it. We have no account database, no ad SDK, no third-party tracker.

  • ▸No HaulLedger servers. No accounts to lose to a breach we never had.
  • ▸Optional iCloud sync via CloudKit — encrypted, your iCloud only.
  • ▸Location data is used only for the trip log and never leaves the device.
  • ▸StoreKit handles payment. We never see a card number.
